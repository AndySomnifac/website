reef-pi

Welcome to the official website of reef-pi - An opensource reef tank controller based on Raspberry Pi. This site provides a comprehensive list of general and build specific guides and important announcements for reef-pi. If you are new to reef-pi and want to build a controller with it, first, figure out what you want to control or monitor with Reef-PI (example: timers, light controllers, ATO etc). After that purchase the required parts and Raspberry Pi. Finally, use the general guides and build guides to create the Reef Pi components you need. Some popular builds include:
  • Power controller
  • LED light
  • Auto Top Off
  • Temperature Controller
  • Touch screen display
